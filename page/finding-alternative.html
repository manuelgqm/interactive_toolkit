Search for alternatives

Several databases include alternatives to the use of dangerous chemicals and tractical experiences of substitution

  • SUBSPORT Case Story Data Base : provides substitution examples as well as information on alternative substances and technologies from enterprises, published reports and other sources
  • Alternativas : (only in Spanish) database developed by ISTAS with documents on alternative substances of low and very low toxicity. It includes links to access those documents, as well as alternative processes/technologies and substitution experiences.
  • This electronic publication on POPs in Articles and Phasing-Out Opportunities prepared by the Stockholm Convention Regional Centre for Capacity-building and the Transfer of Technology in Asia and the Pacific presents information on POPs in processes in identified sectors, availability and assessments of alternatives and substitutes to POPs.

    Annex 1 in this publication can also be useful: Annex 1: Tools and legal frameworks for the identification of chemicals and related gaps for monitoring of products and articles possibly containing newly listed POPs
  • Pesticides alternative: Organic Africa website, a key source for information on organic farming resources in Africa, including the African Organic Agriculture Training Manual and the Organic Farming Resource Directory.
  • PAN (Pesticides Action Network, North America) Pesticides Database - Alternatives to Pesticides
  • Current practice of alternatives assessment practice - a meta review OECD: It summarizes the literature on substitution of chemicals of concern or alternatives assessment.
