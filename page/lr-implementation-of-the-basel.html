Step 1:Implementation of the Basel, Rotterdam and Stockholm conventions

This is the first step in a line of defence – with respect to chemical safety. By sending interim import responses for the 47 chemicals under the scope of the Rotterdam Convention, the country is protected since all other countries will respect the decisions to trade or not in Annex III chemicals.

http://www.pic.int/TheConvention/Chemicals/AnnexIIIChemicals/tabid/1132/language/en-US/Default.aspx

Under the Stockholm convention, Annex A chemicals have to be banned.

http://chm.pops.int/TheConvention/ThePOPs/ListingofPOPs/tabid/2509/Default.aspx

Under the Basel convention, prohibit the trans-boundary movement of hazardous wastes unless authorized (prior informed procedure to be followed); ensure that those involved in hazardous waste management take steps to prevent pollution – environmentally sound management.

http://www.basel.int
