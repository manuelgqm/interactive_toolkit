Moderate Resources

Management of industrial chemicals by countries with MODERATE RESOURCES (countries with transitional economies)

It implies one or two limited resources from the following: financial resources, infrastructures, qualified personnel and restricted implementation of laws and regulations.

GIZ "Practical Chemicals Management Toolkit": hands-on tool for the implementation of practices and a system for the resource-efficient management of chemicals and wastes along the lines of the Strategic Approach to International Chemicals Management (SAICM).
