Health effects

Mutagen

ACCORDING TO GHS

Mutagen level:2: Substances which possible mutagenic effects in humans are of concern. The results obtained from appropriate mutagenicity studies are insufficient to place the substance in the second category.

Endocrine disrupter

Source:Substance listed at Scorecard

Substance from Table 4 from document SEC (2007) 1635, with no or insufficient data on ED effects gathered (Category 3a and 3b), for which there is currently no support for inclusion in the priority list

Neurotoxic

Effect:CHOLINERGIC: Stimulated or transmitted by acetylcholine. Applies to parasympathetic nerve endings. There are changes in the state of consciousness, muscle weakness and excessive secretory activity.

Level: 2: Neurotoxic substances causing measurable biochemical alterations (e.g., at neurotransmitters or activity of enzymes level).

Source:Cabrera R, del Río PA, Mejía MC, Álvarez L, Torrecilla JM Manual de prevención de riesgos en el manejo de plaguicidas Madrid: Fraternidad Muprespa; 2000 (Spanish).
