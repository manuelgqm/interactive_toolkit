Health effects

Carcinogen

ACCORDING TO IARC

Group:3: Not classifiable as to its carcinogenicity to humans. That is, there is no adequate evidence of carcinogenicity in humans and inadequate or limited in experimental animals.

Volume:(VOL. 30, SUPL. 7; 1987)

Endocrine disrupter

Source:Substance listed at Scorecard

Substance from Tables 2 and 3 from document SEC (2007) 1635, with evidence of ED effects (Category 1) or with potential evidence of ED effects (Category 2), which is already regulated or being addressed under existing legislation

Neurotoxic

Effect:CHOLINERGIC: Stimulated or transmitted by acetylcholine. Applies to parasympathetic nerve endings. There are changes in the state of consciousness, muscle weakness and excessive secretory activity.

Level: 2: Neurotoxic substances causing measurable biochemical alterations (e.g., at neurotransmitters or activity of enzymes level).

Source:ACGIH (American Conference of Governmental Industrial Hygienists)
